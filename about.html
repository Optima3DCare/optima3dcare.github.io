Who are we?

Our company name is "Optima 3D Care". We are aiming to make environment-friendly products made via 3D development that will protect health care products from getting damaged by conditions such as heat.

We are introducing our noble product, 'InsuRakshak', a protective case which will keep life saving drugs like insulin in optimal temperature. Medicines and injections are sensitive to heat. High or too low temperature can alter the chemical composition of the drug which makes it unsuitable to consume. It's a compact, easy to carry case which will protect insulin even in extremely harsh heat.
